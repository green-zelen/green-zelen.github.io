Voipscan / iOS / 2017

Voipscan is it messenger App for business chats and for cheap calls. Users may text for many organizations for some services, like shops with delivery, pharmacies, medical centers and other. Don't need to find sites and phone numbers on the internet, just open the organization list and looking for what you need. The registered organizations have a support for this App and reply you in chat.

Also, by this application, you may call anywhere in the world and use text chat in the ordinary messenger mode.

The new version of Voipscan with my design will be released in App Store pretty soon.

Jivu Sport / iOS / 2017

Jivu Sport is an application for sports news and sports events. The App finds you geo position and proposes you available events what you may visit. The user can buy a ticket for an event like a spectator or take part in a competition.

Besides this application work like sports news aggregator. You can find news with filters at your favorite sports and at your location.

Now this App in development and we waiting for release soon.

ServiceMall / Web / 2017

ServiceMall is a website for hiring people for small services, like repairs, cleaning and one-time specific professional work. At the website customers looking for helpers, and helpers looking for customers. Service include tasks manager, produced payment and reviews as well.

People compete for rating and got professional statuses. Website support controls the deals and rule enforcement.

Unfortunately, this website is no sponsored anymore and stopped at development status.

Habar / Android / 2016

Habar is a messenger for ethnic Russian Muslims. They are speaking at different languages, but live and work together in a big country. This application can work like a typical chat, but at more, it helping people with translation other languages and remember praying time.

At development process, I and other developers have researched a lot of ethnic and religion particular qualities. It was a really interesting and specifical halal project.

As I know this App still working with minimal changes at design.
