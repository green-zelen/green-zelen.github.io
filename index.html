Viamme / Web / 2019

Viamme it's the service for people who want to know something about the places where they are traveling, and for people who know something interesting in places where they are living or working.

Guides or just citizens can create their own solo or group excursions, events and master-classes for city guests. Reviews and ratings are the main assessment tools.

Voipscan / iOS / 2017

Voipscan is it messenger App for business chats and for cheap calls. The main feature it's the possibility to text for many organizations or services, like shops with delivery, pharmacies, medical centers, and others. User doesn't need to find sites and phone numbers on the internet, cause he can find everything on the list of services on categories, tags or organization names. The registered organizations have support for this App and reply to chat.

Now this application more popular like call-app. Cheap international calls are the main feature. The chat features are in the development process.

Jivu Sport / iOS / 2017

Jivu Sport is an application for sports news and sports events. The App recognizes the geo position and proposes available events what you may visit. The user can buy a ticket for an event like a visitor or take part in a chosen competition.

Besides this application work like a sports news aggregator. User can find news with filters at favorite sports or depending on location.

ServiceMall / Web / 2017

ServiceMall is a website for hiring people for small services, like repairs, cleaning and one-time specific professional work. At the website customers looking for helpers, and helpers looking for customers. Service includes tasks manager, payment and reviews as well.

People compete for rating and got professional statuses. Website support controls deals and rule enforcement.

Unfortunately, this website is no sponsored anymore and stopped at development status.

Habar / Android / 2015-2016

Habar is a messenger for ethnic Russian Muslims. They are speaking in different languages, but live and work together in a big country. This application can work like a typical chat, but at more, it helps people with translation other languages and with responsibilities of the cult (praying time, religious tradition and education).

At the development process, I and other developers have researched a lot of ethnic and religion particular qualities. It was a really interesting and specifical halal project.

As I know this App still working with the changes at the design from 2016.
